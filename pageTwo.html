Benefits of Clean Solar Panels
Warrenty
Once dirt starts to accumulate, it sticks and can ruin the warrenty on your solar panels because of poor care.
Efficency
When dirt and dust cover your solar panels, they become less effective because they are covered.
Condition
When solar panels are left dirty for long period of time, they start to deteriate quicker and can cost a fortune to fix
